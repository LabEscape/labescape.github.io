About

As one of ESC's research teams you have been assigned to study a newly discovered anomaly. But even a routine experiment can go wrong - and now you find yourself trapped inside the lab.

In the laboratory prepared by students of CTU FIT, something has gone wrong. Your task is to uncover the cause and fix it before it’s too late — only then will you find your way out.

The room is located in the CTU Dejvice campus at Thákurova 9, right in front of the Faculty of Information Technology.

The room is designed for a team of 2 to 4 players and the expected play time is around 30 minutes. However an individual visit is also possible.
